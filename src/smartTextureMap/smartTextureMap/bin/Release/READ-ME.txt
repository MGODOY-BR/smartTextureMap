smartTextureMap - Version Alpha
----------------
Maps the faces in texture map generated from Blender with letters to allows identify them in 3D image and agile the texture process.

Sintax
------

smartTextureMap <<fileName>>

List of Arguments
-----------------
fileName	-	When informed, generates a smart texture map from the original.

	Ex.:
	smartTextureMap test.png

	Generates: test.smartMap.png
